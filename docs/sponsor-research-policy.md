# Sponsor Research Policy

## Allowed Sources

- Company-owned public websites.
- Public contact, partnership, sponsorship, about, and press pages.
- Public press releases.
- Public directories whose terms allow access.
- Manual entries from an authorized user.
- Approved search APIs.

## Disallowed Sources

- Login-protected services.
- LinkedIn scraping.
- CAPTCHA bypass.
- Rate-limit or bot-protection bypass.
- Purchased lead lists.
- Guessed email formats.
- Sources whose terms disallow the intended access.

## Provenance Requirements

Every material fact needs:

- Source URL.
- Retrieval timestamp.
- Source type.
- Confidence.

## Risk Rules

Tier C companies require compliance review and cannot enter an outreach queue automatically.

Tier C includes financial services, sweepstakes, fantasy sports, casinos, betting, alcohol, tobacco, nicotine, hemp, cannabis, crypto, and products marketed primarily to minors.
