# Scheduled Automations

Automations prepare work only. They do not contact sponsors.

## Daily Research Preparation

- Use approved categories and bounded query lists.
- Deduplicate by normalized company and domain.
- Score and risk-classify.
- Save candidates to research review.
- Do not send outreach.

## Daily Follow-Up Preparation

- Find due follow-ups.
- Stop if reply, opt-out, bounce, WON, LOST, PAUSED, or DO_NOT_CONTACT is present.
- Generate draft follow-ups.
- Place drafts in approval queue.
- Do not send.

## Weekly Pipeline Review

Report new prospects, drafts, approvals, sends, replies, meetings, proposals, won/lost deals, overdue actions, data-quality issues, and recommended next priorities.

## Monthly Data Hygiene

Flag stale research, missing source URLs, duplicate domains, bounced contacts, and inactive companies. Never delete records automatically.

## Activation

Do not activate production jobs without explicit approval.
